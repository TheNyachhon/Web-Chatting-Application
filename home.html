ABOUT

Made with utmost care, and keeping in mind of our valuable users, We bring to you HamroCHAT. Hamro in nepali means "Our". It is to describe unity, and togetherness which we plan on bringing with this very application of ours.

Currently pursing 3rd year in our Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering, This project is and will always be of great importance to us. The sole purpose of this creation of ours is to bridge the gap between two individuals by means of communcation.

Initially, We have just introduced a basic chatting module, but we have bigger plans for the future Stay tuned!
